Block Printing

Block printing on textile is a centuries-old technique that involves creating intricate designs on fabric using carved wooden blocks. Originating in ancient China and India, block printing has been practiced across cultures and continents, each with its own unique styles and motifs. The process begins with the creation of wooden blocks, carved with intricate designs or patterns. These blocks serve as stamps, allowing artisans to transfer the designs onto fabric by applying ink or dye to the block and pressing it onto the textile surface.
