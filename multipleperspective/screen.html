Screen Printing

Screen printing on textile, a method dating back to ancient times, has evolved into a dynamic and versatile process for transferring vibrant designs onto fabric. Using a mesh screen, ink is meticulously pressed through stencils to create intricate patterns and graphics on textile surfaces. Screen printing offers unparalleled versatility, allowing for the application of vibrant colors, bold designs, and intricate details onto a wide range of fabrics.
